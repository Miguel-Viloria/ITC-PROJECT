About Us

The Colleges of Engineering, Computer Sciences, and Architecture at Lyceum of the Philippines University are dedicated to shaping future leaders and innovators. Our programs combine academic learning with hands-on experience, preparing students to meet the challenges of today’s dynamic and evolving industries. Through state-of-the-art facilities, expert faculty, and a commitment to excellence, we empower our students with the knowledge, skills, and creativity needed to excel in fields that are essential to society. Whether developing cutting-edge technology, designing sustainable structures, or engineering solutions for a better world, our students are equipped to make a meaningful impact.
