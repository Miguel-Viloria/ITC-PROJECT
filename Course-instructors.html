About Ms Alyssa Paola Pocaan

Program Chair in Information Technology at Lyceum of the Philippines University Cavite
Ms. Pocaan holds a leadership role in the Information Technology program at LPU Cavite. As the Program Chair, she oversees the program’s curriculum, ensuring that students receive high-quality education that prepares them for careers in IT. She also works closely with faculty members and administration to maintain academic standards, manage schedules, and introduce improvements to the program.

Council Adviser for SATA(LPU Student Association for Technological Advancement)
As a council adviser for SATA, Ms. Pocaan serves as a mentor and guide for students who are part of this organization. SATA focuses on promoting technological innovation and advancing students’ skills in technology. Her role includes providing support, offering advice on projects and events, and ensuring that the association aligns with the university’s goals.

Instructor for Capstone Project 2 and Computer Programming 1 and 2
Ms. Pocaan teaches both foundational and advanced subjects in the field of computer science. She handles:

  • Capstone Project 2: This involves guiding students in developing and completing real-world IT projects,
    helping them apply what they’ve learned in class to solve practical problems.
  • Computer Programming 1 and 2: In these courses, she teaches students the fundamentals and intermediate-level programming concepts, including how to write, debug, and maintain code.
